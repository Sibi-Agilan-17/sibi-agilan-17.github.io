Manikandan

Student Delegate — Sir Sivaswami Kalalaya Senior Secondary School, Chennai

Manikandan N is an aspiring Artificial Intelligence and Data Science student passionate about innovation, problem-solving, and impactful technology. He has developed notable projects such as Doctor AI, which aimed to support COVID-19 health management, and Agri AI, a solution for improving agricultural productivity through intelligent insights. During the COVID-19 pandemic, he completed multiple internships online with organizations like OLL, Intel, and 1M1B, gaining exposure to real-world AI and sustainability challenges.

Known among peers as a disciplined and determined learner, he has consistently demonstrated leadership and academic excellence, standing among the top achievers of his school. Beyond academics, Manikandan is a talented and recognised budding percussionist, admired for his rhythm, creativity, and stage presence in various school events.

His vision is to combine AI with sustainability, creating impactful technological solutions that contribute to society and the environment.
