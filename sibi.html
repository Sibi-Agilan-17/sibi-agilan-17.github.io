Sibi Agilan

Student Delegate — Sir Sivaswami Kalalaya Senior Secondary School, Chennai

Sibi Agilan is a Class 12 student at Sir Sivaswami Kalalaya Senior Secondary School, Chennai, driven by a deep interest in artificial intelligence, robotics, and sustainable innovation.

Sibi created SortBot, an AI-powered waste sorting system recognized by 1M1B as the 3rd best project in India. The project was displayed at the National Level Skills Summit at B.S.A. Crescent Institute of Science and Technology, Vandalur, and demonstrated publicly at Chennai Citi Centre, promoting responsible waste management practices.

As part of the Research Science Initiative at IIT Madras, Sibi completed a six-week residential program and presented a research project before eminent professors. Through an internship at 1M1B headquarters in Bangalore, in collaboration with Aditya Birla Fashion and Retail, Sibi gained hands-on exposure to the intersection of AI and sustainability.

As Head of the Tech Team at school, Sibi leads initiatives in robotics and machine learning, focusing on how emerging technologies can be applied to solve environmental and social challenges. With a passion for ethical AI and innovation-driven problem solving, Sibi continues to explore the future of intelligent systems that serve both people and the planet.
