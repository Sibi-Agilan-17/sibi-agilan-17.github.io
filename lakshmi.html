Ms. R. Lakshmi Gayathri

B.Sc (IT), B.Ed (CS), M.Sc (IT), M.Phil (CS)

Teacher Delegate — Sir Sivaswami Kalalaya Senior Secondary School, Chennai

Ms. Lakshmi Gayathri is a passionate educator and novelist with over three years of experience as a PRT in Computer Science and Knowledge Coordinator at SSK. She has mentored over 150 AI student projects, many of which have achieved recognition at national and state levels.

Academic & Research Excellence: She holds university ranks in both UG and PG and completed an M.Phil. thesis on “Proposing a Model for Multiple Object Extraction Using Semi-Supervised Learning Techniques in Video.” Her research contributions include papers on AI and video mining, such as “Extraction of Multiple Objects with Semi-Supervised Learning Techniques” and “Video Mining Using Semi-Supervised Learning Techniques.” Recently, she published “Synergistic Effects of Media, Public Education, and Good Governance” in AIARA Mysore (2024).

Achievements & Recognition: She was honored among India’s Top 50 AI Educators with the Generation AI @ 2047 Educators Award at the AI Teachers Roundtable. Additionally, she was selected by CBSE and IBM to create three video lessons for the Class XI AI curriculum.

Creative Pursuits: Beyond the classroom, Ms. Gayathri is a talented writer whose works include the Tamil novel “Ennai Enna Seithai Venkulale” (Chennai Book Fair 2022), poems like “Meendum Manjapai” and “Neerindri Amayathu Ulagu”, and short stories such as “Mangalm Vanthachu.”

Philosophy: She believes education and writing are powerful tools to shape a compassionate and responsible society. Through her teaching, research, and literature, she continues to inspire learners, foster creativity, and contribute to a better, more empathetic world.
