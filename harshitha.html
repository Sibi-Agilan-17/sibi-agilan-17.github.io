Harshitha

Student Delegate — Sir Sivaswami Kalalaya Senior Secondary School, Chennai

I have a deep passion for music, art, and continuous learning, which have guided many of my interests and achievements. I enjoy playing the piano and have successfully completed the Initial Grade in Electronic Keyboard from Trinity College London. I am also an enthusiastic table tennis player, having secured first place for two consecutive years at the R.K. Swamy Memorial Table Tennis Tournament and participated in matches held at the Nehru Stadium. My love for reading and academic curiosity have helped me achieve the school rank of second in the International English Olympiad, along with awards for academic excellence, particularly in Social Science. In addition to academics and sports, I find great joy in drawing and have won several prizes in competitions organized by my school, the Vaanavil Panpaattu Maiyam, and at the Jainotsav event hosted by Shri B.S. Mootha Senior Secondary School. I was also among the top contestants in the Byju’s Discovery School Super League, Season 5. Beyond these, I have received recognition in music, computer science, art, and sports competitions at my school.
