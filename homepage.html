About Our Project

"Climate Change and Its Impact on Agriculture and Soil Health" — the delegation researches how changing weather patterns affect local crop yields and soil resilience. We combine field observations, low-cost experiments, and community workshops.

What We Present

  • Field trial results on soil amendments
  • Youth-led outreach and curriculum
  • Recommendations for climate-smart practices
