Ananthan Krishnan

Student Delegate — Sir Sivaswami Kalalaya Senior Secondary School, Chennai

Ananthan K is a curious and socially-driven student innovator who works at the intersection of robotics, automation, and behaviour-focused technology. He enjoys building practical systems that encourage better habits, support learning, and make everyday technology more meaningful and accessible.

As part of the 1M1B program, he developed SortBot, a smart waste-sorting and gamified awareness model designed to help school students understand and practise responsible waste management. Through this project, he explored how simple interactive technology can influence behaviour and inspire sustainability from a young age.

Alongside this, Ananthan has strong technical hands-on skills and has been recognized by his school with a Tech Team badge, reflecting his contribution to technical work and problem-solving in school events and activities.

Outside academics and projects, he is an active percussionist, performing in school cultural events and individual programs with enthusiasm and dedication.

Ananthan believes technology should be practical, inclusive, and available to everyone. His vision is to build tools that encourage learning, support sustainable choices, and create equal opportunities for students and communities.
